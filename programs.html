Empowering Youth Through Purposeful Programs

We offer a range of programs designed to equip young people with the skills, confidence, and support they need to thrive. Each program is built around inclusion, growth, and real-world impact.

TechStart

What it does: Teaches basic computer skills, email use, typing, and Microsoft Office.

What you achieve: Digital literacy, job-readiness, and confidence in tech environments.

Duration: 4 weeks

JobReady Bootcamp

What it does: Guides youth through CV writing, job applications, and interview prep.

What you achieve: A polished CV, interview skills, and readiness to apply for jobs.

Duration: 2 weeks

Varsity Pathways

What it does: Helps youth apply to college or higher certificates based on their marks.

What you achieve: Realistic study plans and successful applications.

Duration: 6 weeks

MentorMatch

What it does: Pairs youth with mentors in education, career, or personal development.

What you achieve: Ongoing guidance, goal setting, and emotional support.

Duration: 3 months (ongoing)

Creative Voices

What it does: Offers workshops in writing, blogging, podcasting, and journalism.

What you achieve: Portfolio pieces and storytelling skills.

Duration: 6 weeks

Community Builders

What it does: Engages youth in volunteer projects and civic leadership.

What you achieve: Teamwork, empathy, and pride in giving back.

Duration: 1 month

SpeakUp! Public Speaking Lab

What it does: Trains youth in speech writing, presentation, and debate.

What you achieve: Communication skills and stage confidence.

Duration: 3 weeks

FutureFounders

What it does: Introduces entrepreneurship and business basics.

What you achieve: Business plans and creative problem-solving.

Duration: 5 weeks

Wellness & Identity Circles

What it does: Creates safe spaces for emotional health and peer support.

What you achieve: Self-awareness and stronger social bonds.

Duration: Weekly sessions

Digital Design Studio

What it does: Teaches youth how to create logos, posters, and websites.

What you achieve: Visual design skills and digital portfolios.

Duration: 6 weeks
